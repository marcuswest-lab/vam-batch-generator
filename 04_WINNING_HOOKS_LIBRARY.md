## Winning Hook Patterns (for new hook generation)

### Category 1: Social Proof Shock
Pattern: Show a real customer scenario that creates disbelief/curiosity
- "Check this" (SC7) — HIGHEST PERFORMER
- "Dodged 4k bill" (SC8)
- "Almost paid double" (SC9)
- "Almost paid 3k" (SC82)
- "I almost didn't..." (VHK20)
- **Verbal hook types that work:** Exclamation, Narrative

### Category 2: Price Anchoring
Pattern: Lead with a specific, surprisingly low price point
- "Starting at $1,597" (CHK2/SC1) — ONLY TRUE COPY WINNER
- "City prices" (SC4/SC87/SC88)
- **Verbal hook types that work:** Statement, Label

### Category 3: Savings Quantification
Pattern: Name the exact savings amount or percentage
- "Save up to 50%" (CHK1)
- "Saved 2k" (SC10 — loser, but angle valid with better execution)
- **Verbal hook types that work:** Statement

### Category 4: Cost Exposure
Pattern: Reveal how much others overpay or how movers add hidden fees
- "How movers sneak extra fees" (SC83)
- "I thought it cost more" (VHK22)
- "Didn't realized" (SC84)
- "Wanted to see options" (SC85)
- **Verbal hook types that work:** Question, Conditional, Narrative

### Category 5: Emotional Relief
Pattern: Promise stress reduction or simplicity
- "Stress-free long distance" (CHK3)
- "Moving checklist" (SC5)
- "We handle it all" (SC57)
- **Verbal hook types that work:** Command, Conditional

### Category 6: Humor/Relatability
Pattern: Lighthearted take on moving pain points
- "Flying funny" (SC11) — STRONG PERFORMER
- **Verbal hook types that work:** Narrative, Exclamation
